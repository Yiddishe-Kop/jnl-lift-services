Services

SERVICE CONTRACTS

Lifts are at the heart of almost every modern building and form an important part of both first impressions and a buildings smooth operation. JNL Lift Services offers a range of lift maintenance contracts, from basic routine servicing to fully comprehensive cover.

All of the lifts that we maintain undergo an initial commissioning survey to establish the mechanical, technical and aesthetic status of each individual lift. With this in place, we can advise on potential future issues, should they exist, and benchmark each lift’s performance.

INSTALLATION

We are fully experienced and thorough in our approach, design and recommendations for any lift specification, consulting closely with you and obtaining your agreement before we start ordering any lift equipment for your project.

Our Construction General Manager will work with you, and any third party builders that you may choose, to ensure your project goes smoothly. We are rigorous in our adherence to statutory compliance, health & safety and site preparation. When working in operational buildings, our installation teams take extra care to minimise disruption and inconvenience to occupiers and visitors.

MODERNISATION

An ageing or failing lift can have a huge impact on both the functionality and status of any building. Common indications of a failing lift include frequent breakdowns, high power consumption, increased waiting times, noisy operation and shabby aesthetics.

All of these, or any one of these, can result in a building appearing old-fashioned, uncared for and not living up to its potential. At JNL we can offer you a full refurbishment and modernization, that will make your lift look and feel brand new.

About

JNL Lift Services are specialist designers and installers of high quality and safe solutions for new lifts across a wide range of applications, from lifting people in low and high-rise buildings to lifting cars and vehicles in parking garages. Our highly skilled design team can provide bespoke or standard “out of the box” designs and plans for your elevator and lift requirements within very short lead times. Their flexible and collaborative approach allows them to work with you to optimise designs and meet your project budgetary requirements. Their collective experience, knowledge, and dedication will add value to any project.

Our lift company is also able to offer comprehensive servicing, maintenance and refurbishment options for any elevator. Our skilled and well-qualified technicians are able to carry out preventative servicing and maintenance on all types of lifts, elevators, escalators and moving walkways. When it comes to compliance with local and national laws and regulations, we conduct safety audits of lifts and elevators. Furthermore, we will identify shortcomings, correct faults and arrange for the necessary certifications.
